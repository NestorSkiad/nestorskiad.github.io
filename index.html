About

I'm a photographer based in Grenoble, specializing in portraits, events and concerts. I've had the privilege of working with diverse clients and capturing meaningful stories.

My personal approach results in images that captivate viewers and resonate emotionally while maintaining the highest quality standards.

When I'm not behind the camera, you'll find me playing guitar or riding my bike up the mountains around Grenoble.
